About my Website

Design and Ideology
I wanted to learn and experiment with this new design philosophy called "Neumorphism". This design tries to express depth and 3D sensations through shadows and textures, while keeping a lot of references to the FlatUI Design aestethic.
This design tries to bring back the textures and work of art that was the old Skeumorphism, that really tried to mimic real-life objects and machines, but leaves behind the huge, heavy complexity that Skeumorphism had, while instead joining it with the more common nowadays Flat UI Design, which tries to be simple and clean.

The problem with Flat UI however, is that it can be just too simple and get too basic, to the point that designers almost have lost jobs or interest in working hard on it because a lot of Flat elements are just that - flat. Almost anyone can design a Flat button. It's just a rectangle with a solid color, right? Even Icons themselves, they just got too flat: two or three lines and a solid color and they call it a day. Don't you think this is starting to get boring and lazy?

Yea, that's where Neumorphism comes in. Without loosing the peaceful and clean nature of the Flat UI, it tries to bring back art, and real depth and creativity into UI and Icons.
